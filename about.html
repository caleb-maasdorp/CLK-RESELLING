Who We Are

We are a trio of passionate individuals hailing from South Africa, dedicated to the art of recycling and reselling. Our journey begins with sourcing used AirPods, carefully refurbishing them, and then presenting them to you in their best possible condition.

Our Mission

Our mission is to give new life to pre-owned products, reducing waste and environmental impact. We take pride in providing quality items at affordable prices, making sustainable living accessible to everyone.

Our Products

Our products are then repackaged into fake boxes for shipping, to avoid customs. The cologne is original, we just ship it in a different box because we are not an official supplier. If you're interested in the original packaging, please reach out to us via email: clk.reselling@gmail.com

Negotiation

We believe in fair pricing and are open to negotiation. If you find an item that catches your eye, feel free to reach out to us, and we'll do our best to accommodate your budget.
